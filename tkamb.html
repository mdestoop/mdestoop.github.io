To Kill a Mockingbird Book Cover Redesign

2018 Massasoit Community College

A school project to take a previously read book
and redesign the cover. It was a challenge
to find a new way to visually represent the story
in a new way. This design highlights the conflict
between right and wrong, and Atticus's role
as the lawyer in the case and his struggles.
